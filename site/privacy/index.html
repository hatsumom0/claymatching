What leaves the cave.

Claymatching is an unofficial, holder-made parody community for adults. A wallet login asks for a readable signature, never a transaction, approval, transfer, recovery phrase, or wallet secret.

Account and community data

Supabase processes authentication and stores account identifiers, your profile, selected public avatar and badges, posts, replies, reactions, matches, reports, moderation state, notification records, and Signal-device directory records. Email or Apple may also process the information needed to sign you in. Wallet addresses and eligible-asset snapshots are kept private unless a feature explicitly labels something public.

Wallet and collection checks

When you ask us to check a wallet, the Claymatching Worker queries public Solana or Sui data through configured providers. Read-only Solana previews are self-attested and do not prove ownership. Signed wallet connections prove control only for the signed message and do not grant permission to move assets. User-triggered Collect achievement syncs contact the Claynosaurz Collect service; Claymatching is not affiliated with or endorsed by Claynosaurz.

Encrypted Signals

Signal content is encrypted on your device before it reaches the Cloudflare relay. Device identity material and decrypted message history remain in browser storage on that device. The relay stores ciphertext and necessarily processes routing identifiers, delivery timing, and ordinary request metadata. Encryption protects message contents from the relay, but it does not make abusive or illegal use acceptable.

Browser storage and cookies

Supabase authentication and Claymatching preferences persist in browser storage. The Worker also sets a Secure, HttpOnly, SameSite session cookie after eligible access is established. Signing out clears the active app session; clearing site data removes device-local state and may also remove access to message history stored only on that device.

Other services and images

Cloudflare provides hosting, Turnstile, request logging, and the encrypted relay. Supabase provides authentication and database services. Email delivery and Sign in with Apple involve their respective providers. If you choose a remotely hosted profile or post background, that image host receives a normal browser request and may see IP address and request metadata.

Retention, safety, and choices

Records are retained while needed to operate the community, enforce safety rules, resolve reports, and meet legal obligations. Bans and moderation audit records may outlast visible content. You may disconnect optional accounts, avoid custom images, use read-only access where offered, clear local browser data, or request account deletion through the Claymatching deletion page.
